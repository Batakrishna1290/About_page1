ABOUT US

Rabindranath Tagore

Rabindranath Tagore was a great poet, writer, and thinker from India. He wrote the national anthem of India and won the Nobel Prize for Literature. He believed in peace, education, and human values.

Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose

Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose was a brave freedom fighter who fought against British rule in India. He led the Indian National Army and inspired many people with his slogan “Give me blood, and I will give you freedom.”
